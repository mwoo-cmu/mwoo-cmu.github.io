But there are also three tones that use the same pitches, but are shorter. These are seven (one, but shorter), eight (three, but shorter), and nine (six, but shorter). We don't need to use more numbers to distinguish short and long, because if a sound ends with 't', 'p', or 'k', and the pitch is tone one, three, or six, the tone will be short.
